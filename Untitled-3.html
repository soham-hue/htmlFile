About Mental Health

Mental health is just as important as physical health. It affects how we think, feel, and act. In college, students often face stress from academics, peer pressure, and personal life. It is important to take care of your mental well-being and seek help when needed.

Tips for Good Mental Health

  • Talk to someone you trust when you feel low.
  • Maintain a healthy lifestyle with proper sleep, food, and exercise.
  • Take breaks and avoid burnout from studies.
  • Practice relaxation techniques like meditation or deep breathing.
  • Seek professional help if stress becomes overwhelming.
