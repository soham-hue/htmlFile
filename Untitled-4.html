About Mental Health

Mental health is about how we think, feel, and behave. It’s normal to feel stressed during college, but reaching out for support makes us stronger. Remember, you are not alone — we’re in this together. 💙

“It's okay not to be okay, but it’s not okay to stay that way.”

Self-Care Tips 🌸

  • 💬 Talk to a trusted friend or mentor.
  • 😴 Get 7–8 hours of sleep daily.
  • 🏃‍♂️ Stay active — even a short walk helps.
  • 🧘 Try meditation or deep breathing for 5 minutes.
  • 📖 Take breaks and do something you enjoy.
