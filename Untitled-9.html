What is Mental Health Awareness?

Mental health encompasses our emotional, psychological, and social well-being. It affects how we think, feel, and act. Mental health is important at every stage of life, from childhood to adulthood. Awareness means understanding mental health conditions, recognizing signs of struggle, and seeking help when needed.

This website aims to educate, destigmatize, and provide actionable steps for better mental health practices.

Common Signs & Symptoms

Anxiety

  • - Excessive worry or fear
  • - Restlessness and sleep issues
  • - Physical symptoms like rapid heartbeat

Depression

  • - Persistent sadness or emptiness
  • - Loss of interest in activities
  • - Fatigue and low energy

Stress

  • - Overwhelmed feelings
  • - Irritability and mood swings
  • - Difficulty concentrating
